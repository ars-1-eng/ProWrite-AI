1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and account credentials.
  • Writing Content: Text and documents you submit for AI improvement, including drafts, emails, and other written content.
  • Usage Preferences: Your writing style preferences, tone selections, and feature settings.
  • Subscription Information: Payment and billing information processed through our secure payment processors (Google Play, App Store).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features used, frequency of use, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, and performance metrics to improve our service.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to understand how users interact with our app.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Services: If you sign in using third-party services (Google, Apple), we receive basic profile information.
  • Payment Processors: Subscription status and transaction information from RevenueCat, Google Play, or Apple App Store.
